Intro

With a wealth of experience in Artificial Intelligence and software development, I have honed my expertisein machine learning, deep learning, and network administration.

My professional journey includes an AI Engineer Internship at the University of Bologna, where I bridged Unreal Engine with AI technology tocreate immersive augmented reality experiences.

As a Teaching Assistant at the same institution, I have supported artificial intelligence courses and Python programming modules, fostering a deep understanding of these subjects among students.

My tenure as a Senior Python Developer at Faradid, Iran, saw me consistently exceed targets, innovate practices, and mentor new staff. Adopting a detail-orientedand driven approach, I thrive in dynamic team environments.

I am passionate about contributing to cutting-edge AI research and delivering impactful solutions across diverse industries. My commitment toadvancing innovation in AI is marked by precision and dedication.
